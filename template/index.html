About Bis program

It is the abbreviation for Business Information Systems. It is an academic study program that aims to blend information systems and technology sciences with financial and commercial sciences. The study in the program includes a unique combination of the most important and most important courses that keep pace with the needs of the labor market locally, regionally and globally in the fields of information technology and business.

Available fields of work for a graduate of the (BIS) program

Graduates of the Business Information Systems (BIS) program are distinguished for qualifying them for many different job opportunities in the local, regional and global labor markets. As the program provides a strong background in the commercial fields, and qualifies the learner to work in professions related to information and communication technology, which have become a feature of this era. The graduate of the program also has opportunities that qualify him to work in the following professional specializations in the labor market 1001101001 - Analysis and design of administrative and accounting systems in various economic activities - Design of information networks - Work in technology companies, the banking sector, insurance companies, the tax authority and all parties government - Design and programming of administrative accounting applications and websites - Creation of specialized databases - Specialized electronic accounting

Business Information Systems (BIS) Program Objectives

The Business Information Systems (BIS) program with the credit hour system represents a new vision and mission in university education in public universities to make a quantum leap for public university education in Egypt towards quality and accreditation in colleges with large numbers on the one hand, and to break into the field of competitiveness at the regional and global levels on the other hand. The program seeks to achieve compatibility between the requirements of the labor market by preparing a distinguished graduate for the labor market who is able to mix the disciplines of information systems and financial and commercial sciences
